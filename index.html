Aim and scope

Mathematical modelling and numerical simulations have become a major field of research with a broad range of applications, e.g. in physics, engineering, biology and health sciences.
Their success yields to consider more challenging problems both in the complexity and size, requiring the development of new models and numerical methods as well as efficient algorithms and advanced computational techniques based on appropriate software/hardware technologies.
The aim of the conference is to highlight new technics and recent developpements in this area, and to bring together researchers and experts from the fields that give rise to stimulating problems involving high level modelling and computational environment.
The conference is adressed to a large public from different areas of mathematics, mechanics and engineering.
Young researchers wishing to deliver 25mn talks, are invited to submit a proposal (title and abstract) to the organizers before June 5, 2017. There is no registration fees for speakers and a limited support (accomodation in Mulhouse) will be available.
